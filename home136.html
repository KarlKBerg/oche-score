501/301

501/301 is the most popular darts format, commonly used in professional competitions and pub leagues.

Mickey Mouse

A strategic target-based game where players must hit numbers 20 down to 15, doubles and tripples, plus bull, 3 times to win.

Killer

A competitive elimination game where players first claim a number, then score hits to become a “killer” and knock opponents out.

121 Checkout

A finishing practice game focused on checking out from 121 or lower, helping players improve accuracy and decision-making under pressure.

Score training

A practice mode designed to improve scoring power by focusing on high-value targets like trebles and consistent grouping.

Singles training

A precision-focused game that helps players improve accuracy on single segments across the board.

Doubles training

A dedicated practice mode for mastering doubles, essential for finishing legs in competitive play.

Around the clock

A classic progression game where players must hit numbers 1 through 20 in order, testing consistency and board coverage.
